*Origin: Pernambuco pass. Cross-border tag — gazetteer nodes reference this file; see Instances below.*

## Archetype 1: `metropolitana-nordestina-litoral` (instance: Metropolitana do Recife)

**Identity:** Coastal Northeastern metropolis — colonial port core, reefs and rivers threading through modern towers, dense informal periphery, beach as social space.

**Visual kit:**
- Terrain: flat coastal plain cut by rivers and mangrove estuaries; offshore reef line visible as a dark stripe with breaking white water
- Architecture: pastel Portuguese/Dutch colonial core; mid-rise beachfront wall; warehouse port district; red-tile low-rise sprawl
- Palette: reef turquoise + mangrove green-brown, pastel yellows/blues, white sand, concrete gray accents
- Bird's-eye signature: bridges — the metro reads from above as islands stitched together

**Soundscape/culture:** frevo (umbrellas, brass, acrobatic dance), maracatu nação, manguebeat legacy, Carnival as street event (not stadium)

**Economy hooks:** port trade, fishing, tech/creative district, beach kiosk commerce

**Landmark templates:**
1. Colonial hill district with churches overlooking the sea (Olinda pattern)
2. River-mouth bridge cluster with markets on both banks
3. Reef-protected urban beach with natural pools at low tide

**Image prompt template:** *"Bird's-eye isometric view of a tropical coastal city district, {pastel colonial core | modern beachfront towers}, rivers with bridges, mangrove edges, dark reef line offshore with white surf, red-tile rooftops inland, bright humid light. Game asset style, clear sight lines."*

**Hero city inside this instance:** Recife (full dossier — pending). Olinda is a strong "generic + signature landmark" candidate (the hill-top colonial skyline).
