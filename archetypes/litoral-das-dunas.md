# Archetype: `litoral-das-dunas`

*Origin: Ceará pass. Cross-border tag — gazetteer nodes reference this file; see Instances below.*

## NEW ARCHETYPE: `litoral-das-dunas` (instance: Litoral Cearense)

**Identity:** The windswept dune coast — white sand-dune fields, freshwater lagoons, jangada fishing villages, and a thoroughly 21st-century economy: this stretch is the kitesurf capital of the world (the Cumbuco→Jericoacoara corridor draws a global season crowd) and the heart of Brazil's wind-power boom, with turbine lines crowning the dunes. Reusable across RN and PI coasts; Lençóis Maranhenses (MA) is its natural mega-variant.

**Visual kit:**
- Terrain: rolling white/gold dune fields, interdune lagoons (seasonal blue mirrors), long beaches with constant-wind surf, red cliff sections (Canoa Quebrada pattern), mangrove river mouths
- Architecture: fishing villages of simple bright houses; Jericoacoara pattern — sand streets, no cars, low-key upscale pousadas inside a national park; kite schools and beach clusters; white wind turbines in ordered rows on dune crests
- Palette: white-gold sand, lagoon blues, kite-canopy color confetti in the sky, turbine white, jangada sail triangles
- Bird's-eye signature: dune field + lagoons is already unique; add moving kite canopies over the water and turbine lines — unmistakably now

**Culture (present tense):** jangadeiro fishing tradition (the raft with the triangular sail — historic symbol of fisher resistance) coexisting with international kite tourism; forró beach parties; sunset dune ritual (Jeri's Duna do Pôr do Sol — the whole town gathers to applaud the sunset, a ready-made daily social event)

**Economy hooks:** wind-season tourism cycle (Jul–Jan winds = high season — a seasonal economy driven by weather, pairing beautifully with the sertão's rain cycle), jangada fishing, buggy/kite transport routes along the beach "highway", tension theme: turbine/resort expansion vs. traditional fishing communities' land

**Landmark templates:**
1. Dune-and-lagoon field with kite spots (wind-condition mechanic)
2. Car-free sand-street village inside a park (Jeri pattern — day/night: sleepy → forró)
3. Jangada village beach with fish landing at dawn (daily market event)
4. Sunset dune (scheduled daily social gathering)

**Image prompt template:** *"Bird's-eye view of vast white sand dunes meeting a turquoise ocean, blue freshwater lagoons between dunes, dozens of colorful kitesurf kites over the water, a small village with sand streets and bright simple houses, jangada sailboats with triangular sails on the beach, line of white wind turbines on a distant dune crest, strong afternoon light. Isometric game asset."*

**Signature-landmark candidates:** Jericoacoara, Canoa Quebrada, Cumbuco.
