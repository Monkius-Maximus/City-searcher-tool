# Archetype: `cerrado-agroindustrial`

*Origin: Bahia pass. Cross-border tag — gazetteer nodes reference this file; see Instances below.*

## Archetype 5: `cerrado-agroindustrial` (instance: Oeste Baiano)

**Identity:** The archetype no colonial-era map could imagine — Brazil's western agro-frontier (part of the MATOPIBA soy/cotton belt). Planned grid cities younger than their own inhabitants (Luís Eduardo Magalhães was founded in 2000), mega-farms, and the cerrado biome. Reusable across MT, GO, TO, MA, PI — this single archetype covers most of the Centro-Oeste gap the Brasileirão lens exposed.

**Visual kit:**
- Terrain: vast flat plateau, giant crop circles from center-pivot irrigation (THE most striking bird's-eye signature in the country — perfect green circles on red earth), remnant cerrado (twisted trees, golden grass), buriti-palm spring valleys (veredas)
- Architecture: brand-new grid towns with wide avenues, grain silo clusters (cathedral-scale steel cylinders), machinery dealerships, agro-industry sheds
- Palette: pivot-circle greens on terracotta-red soil, silo steel gray, cerrado gold and twisted dark-green, enormous blue sky
- Bird's-eye signature: pivot circles + silo clusters — instantly readable, geometrically beautiful, unmistakably contemporary

**Culture (present tense):** internal-migration melting pot (gaúcho farmers meeting Northeastern workers — churrasco and forró in the same town), agro-fair mega-events (Bahia Farm Show pattern), frontier-town energy; tension theme: expansion vs. cerrado conservation and traditional/quilombola communities (the real geraizeiros conflict) — rich, genuinely modern quest material

**Economy hooks:** industrial farming loop (planting/harvest logistics at scale — a completely different farming fantasy from the Zona da Mata or cabruca), commodity market prices as a live system, trucking routes

**Landmark templates:**
1. Pivot-irrigation mega-farm with circular field array
2. Grain silo terminal + truck yard
3. Vereda oasis (buriti palms and spring) as the conservation counterpoint
4. Agro-fair grounds (seasonal event)

**Image prompt template:** *"Top-down view of a vast flat agricultural plateau with enormous perfect green crop circles from center-pivot irrigation on red-orange soil, a cluster of giant steel grain silos, straight highway with trucks, patches of golden twisted-tree savanna, immense scale. Isometric game asset, geometric and modern."*

**Signature-landmark candidates:** Barreiras, Luís Eduardo Magalhães.
