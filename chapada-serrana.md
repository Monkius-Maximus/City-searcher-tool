# Archetype: `reconcavo-baiano`

*Origin: Bahia pass. Cross-border tag — gazetteer nodes reference this file; see Instances below.*

## Archetype 1: `reconcavo-baiano` (instance: Recôncavo)

**Identity:** The fertile crescent around the Baía de Todos os Santos — the historic heartland of Afro-Brazilian culture. Colonial towns, yes, but framed by what they are TODAY: living centers of samba de roda (UNESCO intangible heritage), Candomblé terreiros, and riverside fishing economies, plus heritage-restoration tourism.

**Visual kit:**
- Terrain: bay-edge lowlands, tidal rivers (Paraguaçu), mangroves, massapê dark soil fields
- Architecture: baroque church towers over small ports; pastel sobrado rowhouses in varying states of restoration and beautiful decay; saveiro sailboats
- Palette: bay silver-blue, mangrove greens, weathered pastels, dark massapê earth
- Bird's-eye signature: town + river-bend + church-tower pairs repeating around the bay

**Culture (present tense):** samba de roda circles, Irmandade da Boa Morte festival (Cachoeira, August), Candomblé as living religion — respectful representation mandatory, ceremonies not gamified as "exotic content"

**Economy hooks:** artisanal fishing, dendê palm oil, tobacco (Recôncavo cigars are premium exports today), heritage tourism, ferry/boat transit across the bay

**Landmark templates:**
1. Riverside heritage town with bridge and church-front square (Cachoeira/São Félix pattern — twin towns facing across a river)
2. Bay fishing village with saveiro pier and mangrove edge
3. Festival square that transforms during Boa Morte / São João

**Image prompt template:** *"Bird's-eye isometric view of a small historic town on a river bend near a large bay, baroque church towers, pastel colonial rowhouses some restored some weathered, wooden sailboats at a small pier, mangrove and dark fertile fields around, soft golden light. Game asset style."*

**Signature-landmark candidates:** Cachoeira (heritage + Boa Morte), Santo Amaro.
