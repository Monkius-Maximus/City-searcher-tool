# Archetype: `costa-do-descobrimento`

*Origin: Bahia pass. Cross-border tag — gazetteer nodes reference this file; see Instances below.*

## Archetype 3: `costa-do-descobrimento` (instance: Extremo Sul)

**Identity:** Where Portugal first landed in 1500 — but the modern region is defined by three overlapping presences: a major tourism economy (from Porto Seguro's mass beach tourism to Trancoso's global luxury village), the living Pataxó indigenous communities (not historical figures — contemporary people with land rights struggles, cultural centers, and their own tourism ventures), and inland eucalyptus plantation forestry.

**Visual kit:**
- Terrain: red-and-white striped coastal cliffs (falésias), long reef-lined beaches, river mouths; inland: geometric eucalyptus blocks vs. remnant Mata Atlântica
- Architecture: historic hilltop quarter pattern (Porto Seguro's Cidade Alta); the Quadrado pattern — grass square ringed by low colorful casinhas facing a cliff-top church (Trancoso); beach barraca strips; Pataxó villages with cultural centers
- Palette: cliff red-ochre + white, beach cream, casinha brights, eucalyptus blue-green geometry vs. native forest irregular deep green
- Bird's-eye signature: the falésia line — a colored cliff ribbon between green tableland and beach

**Culture (present tense):** Pataxó language revitalization and cultural tourism led by the communities themselves — representation rule: Pataxó NPCs are hosts and protagonists of their own spaces, never scenery; discovery history presented from both perspectives

**Economy hooks:** tourism seasons (high/low), beach commerce, indigenous craft economy (on community terms), forestry vs. conservation tension as a systemic/quest theme

**Landmark templates:**
1. Cliff-top historic quarter overlooking the landing coast
2. Quadrado village square (day: quiet grass square; night: it becomes the social hub — strong day/night state change)
3. Pataxó cultural center and reserve (Jaqueira pattern)

**Image prompt template:** *"Bird's-eye view of a grass village square ringed by small colorful single-story houses, a simple white church at the square's end on the edge of red-and-white striped cliffs, turquoise reef-lined sea below, tropical trees, golden late afternoon. Isometric game asset, serene and vivid."*

**Signature-landmark candidates:** Porto Seguro, Trancoso.
